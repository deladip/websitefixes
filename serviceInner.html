About this plan

1

A family health insurance plan is a cost-effective health plan that offers medical coverage to your entire family at a single premium.

2

You can get coverage for up to six members in the family including policyholder, spouse and up to four children. Some family health insurance plans also allow you to enrol your parents or in-laws.

3

The sum insured is offered on a floater basis, covering all the family members enrolled on the policy. You can avail of coverage for expenses incurred for hospitalisation, pre-and post-hospitalisation and more.

Features

Program duration

1 year

Age of the Insured

From 18 to 65 years old as of the expiration date of the insurance contract

Periodicity of contributions

One time grant

Minimum deposit

From $500

Covered risks

  • Departure from life;
  • Permanent or partial disability (disability 1 ,2, 3 gradation);
  • Injury received during the insurance period;

Beneficiary

The insurance contract is considered concluded in favor of the Insured.

In the event of the death of the Insured, the Beneficiaries are the persons appointed by the Beneficiaries under the contract, in the absence of the appointed person - the heirs / guardians (for minors of the Insured) of the Insured in accordance with US law. For the risk "Departure" - an individual appointed with the written consent of the Insured (his legal representative) in the event of his death, or the heirs of the Insured in accordance with US law.

For other risks - the Insured himself.
